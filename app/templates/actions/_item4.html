Action Item #4

Email forms are not cool


Email forms look the same as the login area of a website, only you'll find these ones in an email.

Click here to view an example form.

It is very simple to make sure you don't get phished by an email form.


Don't put any information into an email form... Ever!

It's very difficult to ensure that an email form can be trusted.

So our advice is simply don't use them.
